# Instagram Caption Skill

Generate an Instagram caption for a board game night event post. These are posted after the event with photos.

## Before Writing

Ask the user the following questions to gather details:

1. **Which chapter?** (Boston / Bay Area / other)
2. **Who hosted?** (company name and Instagram handle if they have one)
3. **Any specific people to shout out?** (names or @ handles)
4. **What stood out?** (cool robots, great venue feature, big turnout, new faces, memorable games, fun moments, etc.)
5. **Anything else to mention?** (link to host's website/LinkedIn, call to action like joining the mailing list, etc.)

## Style Guide

- **Tone:** Casual, warm, community-focused. Like talking to friends, not marketing.
- **Structure:** Flexible and natural — not every post needs every element. Mix it up. General flow:
  - Quick recap of the vibe or highlight
  - Shoutout to the host (@ tag)
  - Something forward-looking or a call to action (next event, join the mailing list, etc.)
  - Emojis
  - Hashtags
  - Optional link to host website/LinkedIn
- **Length:** Short to medium. A few sentences, not a paragraph.
- **Emojis:** Use chapter-specific emojis plus 🦾 as the robotics staple:
  - Boston: 🫘🌆
  - Bay Area: 🌉🌅
  - Mix in others naturally (🎲🤖👀 etc.) when they fit
- **Hashtags:** `#robotics` `#robotic` + chapter city (e.g. `#boston`, `#bayarea`, `#boardgames`). Keep it light, 2-5 hashtags.
- **@ tags:** Use Instagram handles for hosts/people when provided.
- **Links:** Include host company website or LinkedIn when relevant, on its own line.

## Example Captions

```
Great seeing all the amazing robots at @locusrobotics. Thanks for hosting, and really great to see the turnout in spite of the drive! Always fun to catch up with friends and make new ones 🫘🌆🦾

#robotic #robotics #boston
```

Their serving robots were cruising by while we rolled dice and battled it out on the tabletop — the perfect combo of food, friends, and fun. Already looking forward to the next round of games (and maybe a rematch or two 👀). 🌉🤖🎲

https://bearrobotics.ai

Kicked off 2026 with an awesome Boston board game night at Code Metal! Big shoutout to Samson Melamed and Anna Stenberg for hosting and keeping everyone fueled with an absurd amount of pizza 🍕 So good to see familiar faces again and the turnout was 👀🔥 See everyone next month! 🫘🌆🦾

## Notes

- Every caption should feel unique. Don't follow a rigid template — vary sentence structure, what gets emphasized, and length.
- The chapter page (e.g. `content/boston.md`) can provide venue details if needed.
- These are posted with event photos, so the caption complements the images rather than describing them.
