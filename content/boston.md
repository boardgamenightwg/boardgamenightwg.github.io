RASTIC is visible right at street level, on the corner of Commonwealth Avenue and St. Mary's Street. You cannot miss it. Note that map apps drop the pin for 730 Comm Ave on the corner of the building at 16 St. Mary's Street.

Food and drinks are not provided at this venue, so please plan accordingly. Grab dinner beforehand or bring snacks to share. \
This is a BYOB-friendly space, so feel free to bring a beer! \
Games will be provided but feel free to bring your own to share!

Photos and social media are 100% welcome.

## RSVP

## Access
Please do not drive. Take the Green Line to **BU Central**, which is literally at RASTIC's front door.

The front door closes around 8:00 pm. If you arrive after 8:00, just knock on the glass and someone will let you in.

If you have trouble finding us on the day of the event, call RASTIC at (617) 353-3883.
